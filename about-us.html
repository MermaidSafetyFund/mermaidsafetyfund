About the Founders

Mermaid Safety Fund is a small botique firm dealing with 5 or less ventures annually. Our goal is to invest in ideals that show unique or origional potential. Our interests are many but our vision is singular: honesty.

Lawrence T. Murphy


Lawrence is a managing partner and co-founder of Mermaid Safety Fund. He has over 30 years experience in the capital markets and private equity. He began his career working working with some of the largest firms in Wall Street, from Lehman Brothers to Bear Stearns. After completing his education from Boston College and recieving a Bachelor's in economics, Lawrence worked at Lehman Brothers Private Equity Advisors for 15 years in the secondary investment deparment. He later worked at Bear Stearns Asset Management in the pooled investment department.

Truman Weatherly


Mr.Weatherly is a managing partner and co-founder of Mermaid Safety Fund, and graduated from FIU and recieved a Bachelor's in finance. In 1992, Truman Weatherly joined Blackstone Group Private Equity Group until 2007.
